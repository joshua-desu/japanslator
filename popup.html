How To Use :

Ctrl+t

This will convert any selected Japanese word or phrase into readable hiragana.

Magnify

Press "e" to enlarge or shrink the text.

Brighten

Press "w" to bring the widget into or out of focus

English

Click "a->A" to view the text in English.

Lookup

Click "info" to find more information on jisho.org, a Japanese dictionary.

Quit

Press "q" to close the widget
